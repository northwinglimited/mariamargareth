Who We Are

About Maria Margareth Foundation

Maria Margareth Foundation Tanzania is a legally registered nonprofit organization committed to creating sustainable change through education, healthcare, and community development initiatives. Since our establishment, we have been working tirelessly to empower vulnerable communities across Tanzania.

We serve both rural and urban communities by working closely with local leaders, stakeholders, and volunteers to co-create solutions that address root challenges. Our approach is driven by dignity, respect, and the pursuit of long-term impact.

Our Vision

"Quality education for a better life for everyone."

This vision inspires our work every day. To us, education is a powerful tool for equality, justice, and sustainable growth.

Our Mission

We aim to reach the poor, marginalized, and at-risk groups through community driven, voluntary self-help initiatives.

By increasing access to knowledge, resources, and opportunities, we enable people to lift themselves out of poverty.

Our Objectives

Our core objectives reflect our unwavering commitment to sustainable development, equity, and justice, as we strive to create lasting change in the lives of the poor and marginalized. We aim to promote and defend human rights for all individuals, regardless of age, gender, or background, while advancing sexual and reproductive health rights, particularly for women, youth, and vulnerable populations. Through our programs, we equip women and young people with entrepreneurship and vocational skills that empower them to achieve financial independence and build resilient communities. We actively support both national and international volunteering programs that foster solidarity, knowledge-sharing, and cross-cultural collaboration. Recognizing the importance of informed decision-making, we deliver comprehensive life skills education tailored to the needs of vulnerable groups. We also work to establish and promote education for sustainable development, especially in underserved regions, ensuring access to quality learning opportunities. Our health initiatives focus on building strong partnerships with community stakeholders to deliver sustainable health services, while also promoting community-based healthcare, environmental conservation, and tourism policy advocacy. We are committed to encouraging inclusive development through socio-economic and cultural empowerment, backed by impactful research and consultancy to guide evidence-based practices. Furthermore, we provide essential nutrition support and education to malnourished and at-risk children, and use sports and recreational activities as powerful tools to promote health, unity, and positive social interaction across communities.
