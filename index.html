Welcome to Maria Margareth Foundation Tanzania

At Maria Margareth Foundation Tanzania (MMFT), we believe that education is more than a basic right and is the gate way to dignity, opportunity and independence. Guided by compassion, inclusion and empowerment. Our mission is to break the cycle of poverty by offering life changing opportunities to vulnerable individuals and communities.

Through sustainable, community led initiatives, we deliver education, health services and hope to underserved populations across Tanzania. Our work transforms communities one child, one woman and one youth at a time.
